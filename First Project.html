MY FAVORITE FOOD

SUSHI 🍣

For the longest time I did not enjoy sushi, like personally it was thee most disgusting food invented. So when I moved to Cape Town, I would see a lot of people on Tiktok trying out sushi from Linko Restaurant, so I decided to eventually try it out. And you have to start with fried sushi if you are a beginner. So I tried out their Crunch Tempura Roll and honestly it was the best thing ever.

So now I have sushi all the time and best believe I always tell people about the Linko Restaurant so they can also try out the best sushi in town. Period.

DON'T MISS OUT ON SOME GOOD SUSHI!!!
